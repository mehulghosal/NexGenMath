In a \(2 X 2\) matrix, with entries: $$ \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix} $$ The determinant of the matrix is defined as \(ad - bc \). For example, in the following matrix: $$\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 4 \\ -2 & 1 \\ \end{bmatrix}$$ The determinant is \( (1)(1) - (4)(-2) = 1 - (-8) = 1 + 8 = 9 \).
